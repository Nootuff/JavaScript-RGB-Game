The RGB colour model is a method to visualize colour in which different amounts of red, green, and blue light are added together to reproduce a broad array of colours.

See if you can identify & pick the colour displayed in the header from the squares by looking at its RGB values & the different levels of red green and blue they correspond to.

To make the game more challenging, Limited Guess Mode will only give you a few chances, once you run out of guesses, the game is over. To reset the game, press RESET or any of the difficulty selectors.
